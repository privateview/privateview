PrivateView

Your screen. Only your eyes.

How It Works

PrivateView allows your computer screen to appear black to anyone nearby while the real display is only visible through PrivateView glasses.

The Future of Privacy

PrivateView is designed to allow people to work anywhere without worrying about others seeing private information.
